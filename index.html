📖 Hong Kong Mahjong Rules

Objective

Complete a winning hand of 4 sets + 1 pair (14 tiles total).

Sets

Chow (上): 3 consecutive tiles of same suit (e.g., 1-2-3 Bamboo)
Pong (碰): 3 identical tiles
Kong (槓): 4 identical tiles

Actions

• Draw a tile on your turn
• Discard one tile to end your turn
• Call Pong/Kong on any discard
• Call Chow only on left player's discard

Tile Suits

Green = Bamboo (條)
Red = Character (萬)
Blue = Dot (筒)

Discard win: Only discarder pays (×3)
