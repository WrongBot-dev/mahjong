# mahjong
🀄 Hong Kong Mahjong 
Step into the world of Hong Kong–style Mahjong and challenge three AI opponents in a fast-paced, strategy-driven tile game. Play as the South seat and compete against East, North, and West across rotating winds and hands, just like a traditional Mahjong table. Choose your bot difficulty, set your starting balance, and adjust the base bet to control the stakes.

Build a winning hand of 4 sets and 1 pair, call Chow (上), Pong (碰), or Kong (槓) at the right moment, and aim for high-scoring combinations like All Pungs (對對糊), Full Flush (清一色), or All Honors (字一色). With Faan-based scoring, self-draw bonuses, wind and dragon rewards, and real-time balance tracking, every decision matters.

The game runs entirely in the browser with an interactive table layout, animated tiles, round progression, and automatic scoring—bringing the classic Mahjong experience to life in a clean, modern interface.
